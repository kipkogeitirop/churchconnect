2. **No Backlinks** = No Credibility
   - You need 50-100+ quality backlinks

   - Google needs content to rank you for keywords

4. **High Competition**
   - "church management software" = 33,100 searches/month

   - They spend $10,000-50,000/month on SEO

- "church management software with M-Pesa"
- "custom branded church app for small churches"

- "church management system with mobile app"

- **M-Pesa Integration**: No competitor has this
- **Custom Branding**: Highlight white-label solution

- "Church Connect vs Planning Center"

## üö´ Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Keyword Stuffing**: Don't overuse keywords unnaturally
2. **Buying Backlinks**: Google will penalize you
3. **Duplicate Content**: Don't copy from competitors
4. **Ignoring Mobile**: 60%+ of searches are mobile
5. **No Analytics**: Track everything to know what works
6. **Giving Up Too Soon**: SEO takes 6-12 months minimum
7. **Neglecting User Experience**: Fast, easy-to-use site matters
8. **No CTAs**: Every page needs clear call-to-action
