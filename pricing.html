Custom Solutions for Your Church

Every church is unique. We customize the platform with your branding and specific needs.

Below are starting price points - final pricing depends on your customization requirements, features, and church size.

Individual Churches

Starting from KES 500/month, we create a fully customized platform with your church's branding, colors, and logo. Unlimited members, tailored features, and a 1% transaction fee on contributions.

Individual Members

For just KES 50/year, members get individual access with a personal dashboard and mobile app (iOS & Android) to give, view events, and stay connected. Includes a 1-month free trial to explore the platform.

Parish Level

Starting from KES 50,000/year, we provide unified branding across all your parish churches with centralized management, custom workflows, and priority support. 1% transaction fee applies.

Diocese Level

Starting from KES 100,000/year, get complete diocese-wide customization covering all parishes and churches. Includes a dedicated account manager, custom integrations, and advanced features. 1% transaction fee applies.
