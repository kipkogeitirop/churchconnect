About Church Connect

Empowering churches with modern technology since 2020

Our Mission

To empower churches across Africa with innovative digital tools that simplify management, enhance engagement, and foster spiritual growth in their communities.

Our Vision

To be the leading church management platform in Africa, enabling every church to thrive in the digital age while maintaining their unique spiritual identity and mission.

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Church Connect was born from a simple observation: churches needed better tools to manage their growing communities in an increasingly digital world.

Our founders, experienced church leaders and technology professionals, recognized that many churches were struggling with outdated systems, spreadsheets, and manual processes that consumed valuable time and resources.

Today, we serve over 500 churches across Africa, helping them streamline operations, increase engagement, and focus on what matters most - their spiritual mission and community impact.
